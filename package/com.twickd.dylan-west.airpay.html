AirPay - Apple Pay reimagined inspired by AirPod. If you use Apple Pay on daily basis and looking for something stylish and uniform on your iOS device then you have come to the right place.

Features

  • Dark/Light Mode
  • Customize the background color
  • Add border color
  • Change text color

Compatible

  • iOS 13
  • iOS 12 (Has been tested on XS Max)
  • iPhone 6S, 6S Plus, 7, 7 Plus, 8, 8 Plus, X, XR, XS, XS Max, 11, 11 Pro and 11 Pro Max (Not compatible with SE)

Known issues

  • You can't use button to pay with passcode (I will need to find a better method to bring button below the glyph)
