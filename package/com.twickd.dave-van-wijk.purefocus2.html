Your Device. Your Focus.

Purefocus2 supports ios 13+, if you're looking for older iOS support, go to PureFocus

PureFocus2 disables acces to notifications and distracting apps for a certain amount of time so you can focus solely on doing the tasks you need to do.

It does this in an elegant and easy way: just select the apps you want, enter the amount of time, and click "Start Focusmode!".

Purefocus2 also offers a CC toggle for even faster focussing.

If you've bought purefocus, and want purefocus 2, its just 1$ for you. If you've bought purefocus1 in the last 2 months, you'll get this one free
